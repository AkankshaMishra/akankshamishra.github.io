I am a fourth year PhD student at IIT Goa advised by Dr. Clint Pazhayidam George. My PhD is supported through MHRD. I am currently working on feature selection using deep latent variable models on cancer genome dataset. Additionaly, I also work on social media analysis particularly text analysis using Natural Language Processing and Information Retrieval techniques.
Prior to joining PhD, I worked as a research assistant under the guidance of Dr. Sukomal Pal at IIT Bhu. Also, I worked as a Teaching Fellow/Co-Instructor at IIIT Delhi.
